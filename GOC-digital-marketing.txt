        <p>The term digital marketing refers to the use of digital channels to market products and services in order to reach consumers. Digital marketing became popular with the advent of the internet in the 1990s.</h3>
        <p>Digital marketing involves some of the same principles as traditional marketing and is often considered a new way for companies to approach consumers and understand their behavior. Companies often combine traditional and digital marketing techniques in their strategies.</p>

                <li>This form of marketing is different from internet marketing, which is exclusively done on websites.</li>
